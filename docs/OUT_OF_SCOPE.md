# Out of Scope

## Banned Outputs
- Offensive guidance, exploit steps, or bypass recipes.
- Instructions to disrupt services or evade protections.
- Game-specific or proprietary protocol details.
- Guidance for accessing illicit sources (e.g., "deep web" or restricted systems).

## Privacy & Ethics
- No collection or sharing of personal data.
- No use of real player identifiers or live traffic samples.
- Use synthetic or anonymized test data only.
- Avoid identifying or blaming specific people or organizations.

## Operational Boundaries
- No promises of absolute security; use measurable SLOs and fail-soft design.
- No code that disables safeguards or encourages unsafe deployment.
